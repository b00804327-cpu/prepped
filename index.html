Rewrite your professional summary

Your current summary is too generic. Tailor it directly to the role — mention the company name, the specific position, and 2–3 of your most relevant qualifications. Keep it under 4 lines.

The job description explicitly mentions AWS and SQL. Even if your experience is basic, list them with context (e.g. "SQL — intermediate, used in academic projects"). This helps pass Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).
